# Playbook: Twitter/X

How to build presence and engage on Twitter/X without being spammy.

## Rules

- Never buy followers.
- Always add value before promoting.
- Engage with replies and quote tweets.
- Keep promotional content to <20% of total posts.
- Log insights and engagement in docs/intelligence/twitter.md

## Content Pillars

1. **Behind the scenes** - Building in public, progress updates
2. **Insights** - Lessons learned, industry observations
3. **Questions** - Ask your audience for input
4. **Product updates** - Announcements, new features
5. **Engagement** - Reply to relevant conversations

## Tweet Templates

### Build in Public
```
Week [X] building [product]:

- [achievement 1]
- [achievement 2]
- [what's next]

[optional insight or lesson]
```

### Problem Tweet
```
[Describe a pain point your users have.]

We're building [product] to solve this.

[CTA or link]
```

### Engagement Tweet
```
[Ask a genuine question relevant to your audience.]

Replying to everyone.
```

## Launch Day Script

1. Tweet about the launch with link (morning)
2. Share a behind-the-scenes or story tweet (midday)
3. Post results/update (evening)
4. Reply to all mentions and comments throughout the day

## What to Log

Append notable tweets and outcomes to docs/intelligence/twitter.md:
- Tweet link
- Impressions / engagements
- Notable replies or DMs
- Actions taken
