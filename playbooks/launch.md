# Playbook: Product Launch

Step-by-step process for launching a new product or major feature.

> This playbook is meant to be executed in order. Check off each step as you complete it. Do not skip steps.

## Pre-Launch (T-14 days)

- [ ] Define launch goals and success metrics
- [ ] Identify target audience and channels
- [ ] Write landing page copy and get feedback
- [ ] Set up analytics tracking (signups, conversions, source)
- [ ] Prepare email list and welcome sequence
- [ ] Draft press/journalist outreach list (see docs/intelligence/journalists.md)
- [ ] Create launch assets (screenshots, demo video, GIFs)
- [ ] Set up customer support queue and FAQs
- [ ] Internal team review of the product (bug check)
- [ ] Schedule social content calendar

## Launch Week (T-7 days)

- [ ] Post teaser content on Twitter/X and Reddit
- [ ] Send warm-up email to existing subscribers
- [ ] Reach out to early beta users for testimonials
- [ ] Submit to Product Hunt (schedule for 12:01am PST on launch day)
- [ ] Prepare Reddit launch post (see playbooks/reddit.md)
- [ ] Brief all team members on launch day responsibilities
- [ ] Confirm all infrastructure is stable and scalable

## Launch Day

- [ ] Publish Product Hunt post at 12:01am PST
- [ ] Post on Twitter/X (see playbooks/twitter.md)
- [ ] Post on relevant Reddit communities (see playbooks/reddit.md)
- [ ] Send launch email to full list
- [ ] Engage with every comment on Product Hunt within 30 minutes
- [ ] Monitor for bugs; escalate via docs/intelligence/bugs.md
- [ ] Update CURRENT_METRICS.md with launch day numbers
- [ ] Collect and log feedback in docs/intelligence/conversations.md

## Post-Launch (T+7 days)

- [ ] Write post-mortem: what worked, what didn't
- [ ] Log key learnings in CHANGELOG.md
- [ ] Follow up with every person who commented or signed up
- [ ] Update ROADMAP.md based on feedback
- [ ] Send thank-you email to launch supporters
- [ ] Document top feature requests in docs/intelligence/feature_requests.md

## Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Actual |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Product Hunt upvotes | 200+ | - |
| Signups on launch day | 100+ | - |
| Website visits | 1000+ | - |
| Email open rate | 40%+ | - |
| Conversion rate | 5%+ | - |
