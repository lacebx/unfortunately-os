# Playbook: Product Hunt Launch

How to run a successful Product Hunt launch from submission to engagement.

## Rules

- Never buy upvotes or use bots. Trust over growth.
- Always respond to every comment personally.
- Launch at exactly 12:01am PST for maximum time on the leaderboard.
- Do not spam people to upvote. Ask genuinely.

## Pre-Launch Checklist

- [ ] Create/verify Product Hunt account
- [ ] Gather 3-5 hunter relationships or self-hunt
- [ ] Write tagline (60 chars max, no buzzwords)
- [ ] Write description (260 chars for the short one)
- [ ] Create gallery images (1270x760px, first image is hero)
- [ ] Record or link demo video (90 seconds max)
- [ ] Prepare maker comment (post immediately after launch goes live)
- [ ] Set up tracking link for Product Hunt traffic
- [ ] Build hunter/support list (people who will genuinely engage)
- [ ] Schedule DMs/emails to go out at 12:01am PST

## Launch Day Script

### 12:01am PST
- Submit or confirm submission is live
- Post maker comment immediately
- DM warm contacts: "We just launched on Product Hunt. Would love your honest thoughts: [link]"

### Morning (7am-10am PST)
- Post on Twitter/X (see playbooks/twitter.md)
- Post on relevant subreddits (see playbooks/reddit.md)
- Send email to list
- Respond to all comments

### Throughout the day
- Respond to every comment within 15 minutes
- Log feedback in docs/intelligence/product_hunt.md
- Update CURRENT_METRICS.md every 2 hours

## Maker Comment Template

```
Hey PH! [Founder name] here, maker of [Product].

[1-2 sentences on the problem you solve and who it's for.]

We built this because [personal story or insight].

Would love feedback on: [specific question]

Happy to answer anything!
```

## What to Log After

- Final upvote count
- Final ranking (product of the day/week)
- Number of comments
- Signups from PH traffic
- Top feedback themes
- Log all in docs/intelligence/product_hunt.md
