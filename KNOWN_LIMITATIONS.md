## Active Limitations

### No company library yet
**Added:** 2026-07-01  
**Severity:** HIGH  
**Impact:** Users cannot run interviews without company data  
**Status:** OPEN  
**Notes:** Core content build required before public launch

### No user accounts
**Added:** 2026-07-01  
**Severity:** HIGH  
**Impact:** Cannot save interview history, track progress, or build retention  
**Status:** OPEN  
**Notes:** Anonymous sessions work but provide no longitudinal value

### No mobile optimization
**Added:** 2026-07-01  
**Severity:** MEDIUM  
**Impact:** Mobile users likely have degraded experience  
**Status:** OPEN  
**Notes:** Not tested on mobile. Interview UI may be unusable on small screens.

### No shareable results
**Added:** 2026-07-01  
**Severity:** MEDIUM  
**Impact:** Virality blocked. Users cannot share outcomes.  
**Status:** OPEN  
**Notes:** A shareable rejection letter or scorecard would be high-leverage

### No feedback loop from users
**Added:** 2026-07-01  
**Severity:** MEDIUM  
**Impact:** Cannot validate whether simulations feel accurate  
**Status:** OPEN  
**Notes:** Need a "Was this realistic?" mechanism

### Single AI model dependency
**Added:** 2026-07-01  
**Severity:** LOW  
**Impact:** If model provider changes pricing or availability, product breaks  
**Status:** OPEN  
**Notes:** Consider abstracting the AI layer early

### No analytics instrumentation
**Added:** 2026-07-01  
**Severity:** HIGH  
**Impact:** Cannot observe user behavior, drop-off points, or popular companies  
**Status:** OPEN  
**Notes:** Blind to actual usage patterns without this
